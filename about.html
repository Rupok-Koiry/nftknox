What's an "Asset-Backed NFT"?

Tradable NFTs Backed by a Real-World Items

Asset-backed NFTs feature all the characteristics of traditional NFTs, but with each minted NFT backed by it's equivalent, real-world object. Held securely in storage, these real-world items are redeemable in exchange for sending the item's NFT back to NFT Knox and securely signing your mailing address from the wallet holding the NFT.
